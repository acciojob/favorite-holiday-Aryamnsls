Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light." It is famous for its iconic landmarks, rich culture, and delicious cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower - The symbol of France with stunning views.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A famous avenue for shopping and cafes.
  5. Seine River Cruise - A romantic boat tour through the heart of Paris.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissant - A buttery, flaky pastry.
  • Ratatouille - A classic vegetable dish from France.
  • Crème Brûlée - A creamy custard with a caramelized top.
  • Macarons - Delicate almond-based cookies.
  • French Onion Soup - A flavorful soup topped with cheese.
